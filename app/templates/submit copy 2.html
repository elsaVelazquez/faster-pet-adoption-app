This app is intended for dog shelters showcasing adoptable dogs.
The app uses Machine Learning models to evaluate the effectiveness of the dog's description.

Please input the dog's description and hit submit.

This application consumes your description of a dog and tells you:
1. The likelihood the dog will get adopted based on your description.
2. How similar your description is to other adopted and not yet adopted dogs' descriptions.
3. The sentiment your description invokes.
